Two Women with a Different Background Fighting for the Same Causes: The Abolition of Slavery and Women's rights May 6, 2021 Women played a significant role in the fight against slavery and racial injustice. Angelina Grimke, a southerner aristocrat and Sojourner Truth, a former slave were both prominent figures in the 19th century. Despite their differences, both fought for the same issues; racial and gender inequality. Background Angelina Grimke Angelina Grimke Weld was born on February 20, 1805, in Charleston, South Carolina to a wealthy slave-holding family.
